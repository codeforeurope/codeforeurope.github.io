We're on a mission to make government
work in the digital age.

Code for Europe is a network of developers and designers that create digital services for cities and communities.

We do this in order to solve real, human problems in a reusable, flexible and inclusive way. We empower local communities to build, share and adapt civic tech. We solve local challenges by profiting from the shared experience of an international network of likeminded fellows.

For cities and other governments

Coders can help cities and other governments collaborate bottom-up in innovation. Code for Europe promotes and facilitates collaborative in-house development of software solutions for governments. We connect people to share knowledge, experience and code between cities.

Fellowships

A small team of experienced designers and developers that work within government; finding problems, creating innovative solutions and educating the government workforce on technology.

Support a brigade

An active community of coders working on apps and data services that create value to the people living in your city. We are working on a whitepaper on how to support these brigades. Interested?

Want to join a brigade?

Volunteer in a project that serves communities “glocally”. Brigades create solutions for their local communities, while at the same time sharing code and collaborating with other brigades around the globe.

We are working on a brigades map. In the mean time, please contact us by e-mail.
